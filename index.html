The Project :
Facial expression classifier.

The Goal was to train a deep learning model to classify facial expressions.

This uses OpenCV’s Haar cascades to detect and extract the facial region from the
webcam video feed and then displays the corresponding emoji (one of 7 classes)
depending on the output from the trained model.

The Kaggle Dataset :
Sale Price prediction of houses in Ames, Iowa

A real estate agent might be able to do this based on intuition and experience,
but a computer – lacking both – might just accomplish the same.

The data contained 81 columns, each describing a feature of the property like building type,
size, no. of fireplaces, neighborhood etc. The aim was to train a model to predict the sale price
of houses based on the given parameters. I learnt a a lot about data pre-processing,
deciding which features to keep and which ones to drop, handling categorical values etc.

The Wine-quality dataset :
Prediction of wine quality

A wine critic is a person who evaluates wine and describes it with a numerical rating. What if we could train a computer to do the same?

The dataset contains 12 columns, 11 of which describe the chemical properties of wine,
the last column corresponds to its quality. The quality of wine is directly correlated to its chemical composition,
these chemical attributes can be used to train a deep learning model to predict its quality.
